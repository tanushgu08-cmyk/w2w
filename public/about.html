About This Program

WasteToWealth is a community-driven platform created to reduce waste and support people in need. Every day, large amounts of food are thrown away while many individuals and families struggle to access basic resources. Our platform connects restaurants, hostels that have surplus resources with NGOs, shelters, and communities who need them. By building this bridge, we ensure that valuable resources are redistributed instead of wasted. WasteToWealth turns excess resources into opportunities for those who need them the most.
