How WasteToWealth Works 🌱

WasteToWealth connects donors, volunteers, and communities to ensure that surplus resources are redistributed instead of wasted.

Step 1 – Donor Adds Resources

Restaurants, hostels, and households can list surplus food or other usable resources on the platform.

Step 2 – Volunteers Get Notification

Nearby volunteers receive pickup requests and accept the task.

Step 3 – Pickup and Delivery

Volunteers collect the resources and deliver them to NGOs, shelters, or families in need.

Step 4 – Impact Tracking

The platform tracks meals saved, food redistributed, and community impact in real time.
